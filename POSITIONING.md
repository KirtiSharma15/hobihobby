# HobiHobby Positioning Document

## Target User

**Urban adults (25-45)** feeling bored, burnt out, or seeking creative fulfillment outside work.

### User Characteristics
- Working professionals with limited free time
- Seeking meaningful, calming activities outside of screens
- Want to learn something new but overwhelmed by options
- Value quality guidance over quantity of content
- Prefer structured paths over endless exploration

---

## Problem Statement

> "I want to start a hobby but don't know where to begin, what supplies I need, or who to trust for guidance."

### Pain Points
1. **Overwhelm**: Too many hobby options, no clear starting point
2. **Trust**: Hard to find reliable, quality guidance for beginners
3. **Cost uncertainty**: Don't know what to buy or how much to spend
4. **Time commitment**: Unclear how much time hobbies actually require
5. **Isolation**: Learning alone without community support

---

## Primary Vertical: Art & Craft

Focus exclusively on creative, hands-on hobbies:

### Phase 1 Hobbies
1. **Watercolor Painting** - Accessible, calming, low barrier to entry
2. **Pottery & Ceramics** - Tactile, meditative, produces tangible results
3. **Calligraphy & Lettering** - Structured practice, clear progression

### Why Art & Craft?
- Low physical energy requirement (accessible to most)
- Tangible outputs (sense of accomplishment)
- Calming, meditative nature (stress relief)
- Clear skill progression (visible improvement)
- Reasonable cost to start ($30-100)
- Can be done at home with minimal space

---

## Success Definition (3 Months)

### Quantitative Metrics
- **100 users** actively exploring hobbies
- **20+ users** completing first 3 steps of any hobby
- **50%** of visitors save at least one hobby
- **Average session time** > 2 minutes

### Qualitative Metrics
- **10 user interviews** with actionable feedback
- Users describe the app as "calming" and "helpful"
- Users feel confident about starting a hobby after using the app

---

## Core Value Proposition

**"Discover your perfect creative hobby with clear, beginner-friendly guidance."**

### Key Differentiators
1. **Curated, not crowded** - Quality over quantity
2. **Clear starting points** - Know exactly what to buy and do first
3. **No pressure** - Explore at your own pace, no streaks or gamification
4. **Trust-first** - Verified instructors and honest recommendations

---

## What We Are NOT

- A social media platform
- A marketplace for hobby supplies
- A gamified learning app
- A course platform with video libraries
- A community forum

---

## Guiding Principles

1. **Discovery over completion** - Let users explore without pressure
2. **Trust over growth** - Quality creators, honest recommendations
3. **Calm over engagement** - No dark patterns, no FOMO
4. **Validation over features** - Prove value before building more

---

## Phase 1 Focus (Discovery-First MVP)

### What We Build
- Browse Art & Craft hobbies by difficulty, time, budget
- Hobby detail pages with:
  - What it is and who it's for
  - Starter checklist with cost estimates
  - First 3 beginner steps
  - Intro video (YouTube embed)
- Save/shortlist hobbies (local storage, no login required)

### What We DON'T Build Yet
- User accounts and authentication
- Full learning paths with modules
- Progress tracking
- Community features
- Payments or monetization
